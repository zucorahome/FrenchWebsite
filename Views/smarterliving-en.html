How does it work?

STEP 1

SELECT YOUR PLAN

With a variety of plans to choose from, you can find the right plan to fit your budget and lifestyle. Select an appliance, furniture, or home systems plan. Save by bundling plans or select the Whole Home Plan for a complete coverage solution.

STEP 2

REQUEST ASSISTANCE

Should a covered item require service, we’re ready to help. Contact us by phone or 24/7 online. If a service technician is required, you’ll pay a small Trade Service Fee and we’ll arrange for priority service with no additional cost.

STEP 3

PROBLEM SOLVED

We’ll be with you every step of the way until your Service Request is resolved. A professional, local contractor will diagnose the problem and offer a solution. We pay for parts, labour, or replacement of the covered item. While you protect your budget.
